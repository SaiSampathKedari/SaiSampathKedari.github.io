Teaching Experience

I served as a Graduate Student Instructor (GSI) for core undergraduate physics courses at the University of Michigan from Fall 2022 to Winter 2024, supporting labs, discussion sections, and exams.

  • PHYSICS 241: General Physics II (Electromagnetism and Special Relativity)
    Fall 2022 (FA 2022), Winter 2023 (WN 2023) — Prof. Lorenzon
    Topics: Electric & Magnetic Fields, DC/AC Circuits, Lorentz Force, Faraday’s Law, and Special Relativity.
  • PHYSICS/BIOPHYS 151: Introductory Physics Lab for Life Sciences
    Spring 2023 (SP 2023)
    Topics: Scaling, Statistics, Mechanics, Fluid Dynamics, and Biophysics applications (e.g., blood pressure, calorimetry).
  • PHYSICS 360: Thermodynamics, Waves, and Special Relativity
    Fall 2023 (FA 2023) — Prof. Jianming Qian
    Winter 2024 (WN 2024) — Prof. Lu Li
    Topics: Thermodynamics, Wave Phenomena, Geometric Optics, and Special Relativity.
