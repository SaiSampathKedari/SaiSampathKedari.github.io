Teaching Experience

I served as a Graduate Student Instructor (GSI) for core undergraduate physics courses at the University of Michigan from Fall 2022 to Winter 2024, supporting labs, discussion sections, and exams.

  • PHYSICS 241 – Electromagnetism & Special Relativity
    FA22, WN23 — Prof. Wolfgang Lorenzon
    Hands-on labs covering fields, circuits, induction, and relativity fundamentals.
  • PHYSICS/BIOPHYS 151 – Physics for Life Sciences
    SP23
    Experimental modules on fluid dynamics, mechanical advantage, and biological measurements.
  • PHYSICS 360 – Thermodynamics, Waves, and Relativity
    FA23 — Prof. Jianming Qian, WN24 — Prof. Lu Li
    Discussion sections on thermodynamics, wave optics, and special relativity.
